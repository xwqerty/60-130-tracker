60-130 Tracker

Privacy Policy · last updated 2026-07-12

The short version: your data stays on your phone. No accounts, no tracking, no ads, and nothing is sent to us.

What the app uses

Location (GPS). The app uses your device's GPS speed to time runs and to calibrate the vehicle-speed reading for accuracy. Location is processed entirely on your device in real time and is never transmitted, stored off-device, sold, or shared. You can revoke Location access at any time in iOS Settings; the app still works with an OBD adapter, just without GPS calibration.

Local network / Wi-Fi. When you use an adapter, the app talks directly to the OBD/ENET adapter on your local network to read vehicle speed. This communication is between your phone and the adapter only; nothing is sent to us or any third party.

What is stored, and where

Run logs (CSV files and a results list) are saved only on your device, in the app's own storage, and are visible to you in the Files app. You can delete them at any time from within the app. A few on-device settings (connection mode, calibration factor, preferences) are also kept locally and never leave the device.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising identifiers, and no servers that receive your data. We do not know who you are or that you are using the app.

Third parties

The app integrates no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and shares data with no one.

Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no personal information from anyone.

Safety

60-130 Tracker is intended for use on a closed course, track, or private property. Never operate it while driving. You are responsible for safe and lawful use. Timing results are estimates provided for entertainment and are not guaranteed to be accurate.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will change, and material changes will be reflected in an app update.
