# Privacy Policy — 60-130 Tracker

_Last updated: 2026-07-12_

60-130 Tracker is built to respect your privacy. The short version: **your
data stays on your phone.** There are no accounts, no tracking, and no ads.

## What the app uses

- **Location (GPS).** The app uses your device's GPS speed to time runs and to
  calibrate the vehicle-speed reading for accuracy. Location is processed
  **entirely on your device in real time** and is **never transmitted, stored
  off-device, sold, or shared.** You can revoke Location access at any time in
  iOS Settings; the app still works with an OBD adapter, just without GPS
  calibration.
- **Local network / Wi-Fi.** When you use an adapter, the app talks directly to
  the OBD/ENET adapter on your local network to read vehicle speed. This
  communication is between your phone and the adapter only; nothing is sent to
  us or any third party.

## What is stored, and where

- **Run logs** (CSV files and a results list) are saved **only on your device**,
  in the app's own storage, and are visible to you in the Files app. You can
  delete them at any time from within the app.
- The app keeps a few **on-device settings** (your chosen connection mode,
  calibration factor, and preferences). These never leave the device.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** The app has no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising
identifiers, and no servers that receive your data. We do not know who you are
or that you are using the app.

## Third parties

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking
SDKs. It does not share data with anyone.

## Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no personal information from
anyone.

## Safety

60-130 Tracker is intended for use on a closed course, track, or private
property. Never operate it while driving. You are responsible for safe and
lawful use. Timing results are estimates provided for entertainment and are not
guaranteed to be accurate.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Material
changes will be reflected in an app update.
